ANIMATION 3D

After a year in the Art & Design preparatory program, I discovered a passion for a new medium: 3D software. Self-taught, I learn to work with color, textures, light, movement, shapes, proportions, and illustration. I now have the compulsive eye of an observer on the world.

My personal universe unfolds as I explore, with an artistic approach, subjects that intrigue, worry, amuse, or challenge me.

ARTISTIC LATHING

Artistic turning is a craft where one learns to machine or manually shape a rotating metal piece on a lathe, as well as various techniques in assembly, forging, and chiseling. It is a rare craft focused on mastering curves, proportions, and profiles. It requires technical and scientific knowledge of various materials, as well as precision in execution. The mind and the hand.

ABOUT ME

My name is Jules. I'm French, but I grew up in Germany, Brazil, and the Czech Republic.

Naturally curious, I love discovering new things and meeting inspiring people.

I have taught myself and by 2 years in Engineering school, various technologies, including programming, electronics, 3D animation, modeling, mapping, and artificial intelligence.

Yet, in constant search of exploration, a need of imagination and to stay connected with the rigor material reality require, I joined the Art Bronze Lathing program at École Boulle.

This discipline blends ingenuity and a sensitive interaction with metal, wood, plastic, through the art of precision machining.

My inspirations revolve around utopias, hope, colors, perception, emotional connections with objects, animism, and the transformation of digital interfaces.

I aspire to embody these ideals in my work, but I know I still have a journey of questioning and evolution ahead of me.
